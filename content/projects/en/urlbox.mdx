Urlbox is a Screenshot service that has grown and evolved over years, now being the best product for website screenshots on the market. With this continuous growth, more and more content has been added to the website. With no design system and structure in place, the website got unstructured over time.

We restructured the whole website, **developed new modern and detailed design and built the site out** using Next.js. The new site includes useful examples, playgrounds and explanations of Urlbox' great features.
